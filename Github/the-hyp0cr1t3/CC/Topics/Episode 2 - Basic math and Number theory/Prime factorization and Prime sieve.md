## Prime sieve
The sieve of Eratosthenes is one of the most efficient ways to find all primes upto N in **O(N.log(log N))** time and **O(N)** space.

[Tutorial](https://cp-algorithms.com/algebra/sieve-of-eratosthenes.html)
